Domain

Our Research Domain

To address the growing challenges in protecting natural ecosystems and ensuring public safety, this research initiative aims to lead the way in developing an AI-driven solution that comprehensively addresses these issues. The primary objective of this study is to create and implement an integrated system that utilizes the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Internet of Things (IoT) technology. This system will have various capabilities, including real-time detection of weapons and accurate localization of gunshots, continuous monitoring of endangered animals, and early identification of forest fires.

Monitor Endangered Animals and Calculate the Density

In the pursuit of preserving biodiversity and protecting endangered species, real-time data has become a valuable asset. Leveraging data obtained from cameras, we have the capability to not only monitor the habitats of endangered species but also perform critical analyses. By identifying the high-density areas of these precious creatures and determining their population counts, we can gain insights that enable us to make more informed conservation decisions.

Objectives of the research component:

  • Analyze the density by image processing
  • Send data to the main server
  • Mark location on the map.​
  • Show the path to reach them.​

Real-time Forest Fire Detection System

Forest fires have become an increasingly common threat to important resources, with devastating effects on the environment and the wildlife that depend on them. Sri Lanka, in particular, is prone to forest fires due to its hot and dry climate, which creates ideal conditions for fires to start and spread quickly. These fires are often caused by human activities such as hunting, agricultural practices, and arson. They not only destroy large areas of forest but also pose a serious threat to the endangered species that inhabit them, further endangering their already vulnerable populations.

Objectives of the research component:

  • Build a 360-degree rotating camera system that can cover a wide range of the forest area and can detect fire points using the flame detector IR modules.
  • Establish wireless communication between the sensors and the main system using LoRa technology to transmit the fire point data.
  • Analyze the forest fire by image processing
  • Send an alert message to the nearest wildlife office with the location of the camera, pointed sensor location

Real-time gunshot detection and localization system

Between January 1st and July 31st of this year, Sri Lanka's Department of Wildlife Conservation documented a distressing total of 107 elephant fatalities. Shockingly, more than half of these deaths, precisely 51, were attributed to gunshots, as indicated in the subsequent report. This alarming statistic doesn't stand alone—highlighting a grim reality where even a full-grown male leopard fell victim to a fatal shooting back in 2019. These numbers underscore a critical and pressing issue: the existing anti-poaching measures are proving insufficient in safeguarding endangered wildlife. The urgency for new, advanced measures, bolstered by technology, becomes apparent, essential to protect these majestic and vulnerable creatures from further harm and potential extinction.


Objectives of the research component:

  • Develop a gunshot detection model.
  • Detect gunshots and localize them.
  • Display alert and mark the location of the gunshot in the app.

Weapon detection system to identify the presence of poachers

Leveraging the power of artificial intelligence to detect and signal the presence of weapons in protected areas marks a revolutionary step in the conservation of endangered animals. By deploying AI-driven systems, park rangers can receive instantaneous alerts, pinpointing the location and timing of potential poaching incidents. This cutting-edge technology acts as a critical tool in the ongoing battle against illegal hunting, enabling swift responses to protect vulnerable wildlife.

Objectives of the research component:

  • Develop AI algorithms that can accurately detect and recognize weapons in real-time using image and video analysis.
  • Create an alert system that triggers notifications to Park Rangers when a potential weapon is detected.
  • Ensure the system records the precise time when a weapon is detected, enabling rapid response.
